City of Cedar Hills

Irrigation Calculator

Cedar Hills has installed pressurized irrigation (PI) water meters on customer connections to comply with State of Utah mandates, encourage water conservation, preserve a reliable water supply, and increase equity in billing.

A tiered rate structure based on usage will allow the City to more equitably distribute system costs among users based on actual water use. The intent of this rate structure is to more equitably distribute costs among users and discourage wasteful use.

Cedar Hills has created an irrigation calculator to help customers estimate the cost of their bill. Enter your lot size to determine the base monthly payment and estimate what your bill would be at different water usage amounts.

Don't know your lot size? Use the Utah Parcel Map to look it up.
